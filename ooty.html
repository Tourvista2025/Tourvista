Ooty

Originally occupied by the Toda people, the area came under the rule of the
East India Company at the end of the 18th century.
The economy is based on tourism and agriculture, along with the manufacture
of medicines and photographic film. The town is connected by the
Nilgiri ghat roads and Nilgiri Mountain Railway. Its natural environment
attracts tourists and it is a popular summer destination in 2011.
