Amritsar

What is story behind Amritsar? Amritsar was founded by Sri Guru Ramdass ji,
the fourth guru of the Sikhs in about 1574 A.D.
Before the city was founded, the area was covered with
thick forests and had several lakes.
One of the most pristine cities with Guru-vaani humming from the Gurudwaras,
Amritsar is renowned world over for the Golden Temple, also known as
Harmandir Sahib Gurudwara - the holiest Gurdwara.
It's at the end of a causeway, surrounded by the sacred
Amrit Sarovar tank (lake), where pilgrims bathe.
