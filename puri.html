PURI

Puri is a coastal city and a municipality in the state of Odisha in eastern India.
It is the district headquarters of Puri district and is situated on the Bay of Bengal,
60 kilometres (37 mi) south of the state capital of Bhubaneswar.
It is also known as Sri Jagannatha Dhama after the 12th-century Jagannath Temple located in the city.
For most Jagannath Temple is the main reason to visit Puri.
Most Bengalis and local people are more interested in visiting
the temple rather than beaches of Puri. The temple is a
century old construction which has undergone some renovation.
