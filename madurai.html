Madurai

Mudumalai National Park is a national park in the Nilgiri Mountains in Tamil Nadu,
south India. It covers 321 sq km (124 sq mi) at an elevation range of 850 - 1,250 m
(2,790-4,100 ft) in the Nilgiri District and shares boundaries with the states of Karnataka and Kerala.
The city has a number of historical monuments, with the
Meenakshi Temple and the Thirumalai Nayak Palace being the most prominent.
Madurai is an important industrial and educational hub in South Tamil Nadu.
The city is home to various automobile, rubber, chemical and granite manufacturing industries.
