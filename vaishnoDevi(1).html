VaishnoDevi

Vaishno Devi (also known as Durga, Mata Rani, Trikuta, Ambe and Vaishnavi) is a Hindu goddess
who is considered as a manifestation and form of the Supreme Mother Goddess.
Durga also referred to as Adi Shakti in Hinduism.
The words "Maa" and "Mata" are commonly used in India for mother,
and thus are often heavily used in connection with Vaishno Devi.
Vaishnavi was formed from the combined energies of Kali, Lakshmi, and Saraswati
having the principal energy of Durga overall.The temple dedicated to her is located in Katra, India.
Vaishno Devi is most holy place in India , and no other city is Holy in the world like it.
Its attractions and places of interest are countless and cater for all tastes and all ages,
its temples and hill view from the mountain are often unforgettable,
its culture - restaurant and sacred places combined - in a class of its own.
