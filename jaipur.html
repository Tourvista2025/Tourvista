Jaipur

Jaipur formerly Jeypore, is the capital and largest city of the Indian state of Rajasthan.
As of 2011, the city had a population of 3.1 million,
making it the tenth most populous city in the country.
Jaipur is also known as the Pink City, due to the dominant colour scheme of its buildings.
It is also known as the Paris of India, and C. V. Raman called it the Island of Glory.
After independence in 1947, Jaipur was made the capital of the newly formed state of Rajasthan.
Jaipur is a popular tourist destination in India and forms a part of the west Golden Triangle.
It also serves as a gateway to other tourist destinations in Rajasthan such as Jodhpur.
